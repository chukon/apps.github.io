Install iOS OR Android Beta App on your phone

iOS USERS

From your iPhone point your camera to below QR Code
OR Click on this link

1) Allow webpage to load
2) Tap Install prompt to install app
3) Allow a minute for SpeakCODE to install
4) From iPhone go to Settings > General > Device Management > Rappidly Inc
5) Tap on Trust "Rappidly Inc" & Trust
6) Go back to iPhone home screen, search and open "SpeakCODE"
7) Confirm you were able to install app on your phone

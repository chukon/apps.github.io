ANDROID USERS

From your Android phone Click on THIS LINK


1) in the Settings app, depending on the device and Android version, it could be in Security or in Applications, check the box "Unknown sources".
2) Once the webpage loads click on the download application link under DOWNLOAD
3) Follow prompts to download and install app
4) Allow time for SpeakCODE to download and install
5) Once completed accept prompt to open "SpeakCODE"
